Welcome to the Ethiopian Election Board's Online Voting System Landing Page!

We are proud to introduce our state-of-the-art online voting system, designed to provide a safe, secure, and efficient way for eligible Ethiopian citizens to cast their votes in elections. With our user-friendly platform, voters can easily register, access their ballots, and securely submit their votes from the comfort of their own homes.

Our team of experienced developers and election experts have worked tirelessly to ensure that our online voting system meets the highest standards of security and reliability. We understand the importance of conducting free and fair elections, and our system is designed to prevent fraud, protect privacy, and provide a transparent and auditable voting process.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more about our online voting system and how it can help shape the future of democracy in Ethiopia. Thank you for choosing the Ethiopian Election Board's Online Voting System.

Political Parties

‘’Political Party’’ means an association that is organized by citizens and is a registered entity according to Proclamation No. 1162/2011. A political party formulates political programmes at the national, regional or sub-regional levels in order to gain political power at the national level. National Electoral Board of Ethiopia Establishment Proclamation 1133/2019

The National Electoral Board of Ethiopia is an institution established in accordance with the constitution of the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia and the new proclamation is cited as the “National Electoral Board of Ethiopia Establishment Proclamation No. 1133/2019”. This Proclamation has come into force on April 1, 2019 upon approval by the House of People’s Representatives amending the previous electoral law Proclamation No. 532/1999.

The Ethiopian Electoral, Political Parties Registration and Election’s Code of Conduct Proclamation 1162/2019

The law governing election registration and related conduct of political parties is called the "Ethiopian Electoral, Political Parties Registration and Electoral Code of Conduct No. 1162/2011". This law came into force on the 24th of August 2019 by repealing and consolidating into one comprehensive legal document three separate laws, namely the Electoral Law of Ethiopia Proclamation No. 532/2007, Political Parties Registration Proclamation No. 573/2008 and Electoral Code of Conduct for Political Parties Proclamation No. 662/2009.

Speed and Efficiency

Online voting systems can be faster and more efficient than traditional paper-based voting systems, allowing for quicker tabulation of results and potentially reducing the time it takes to declare a winner.

Increased Participation

One additional advantage of online voting systems is that they have the potential to increase voter participation, particularly among younger voters who are more likely to be comfortable with technology and online processes. This can lead to a more representative and inclusive democratic process.

price_change
Cost Savings

Online voting systems can save money compared to traditional paper-based voting systems. They can reduce printing, transportation, storage, and staffing costs associated with traditional voting methods.

devices
Accessibility

Online voting systems can be accessed from a variety of devices, including computers, tablets, and smartphones, making it easier for voters to participate in the voting process regardless of their location or the device they have access to.
